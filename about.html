Introduction

Falcon Cycling is a local company based in north east of England, Newcastle. Our stuff is a mix of bunch with volunteers of all ages.

This application will help you in order to to view and record bike safety checks, which after record your bike details, you will be able to view them again. The app also will allow you to view bike categories and description of each bike in the category.

The app will promote a better physical and mental health through cycling, it also promotes the important of safe use and reuse, as well as maintening bikes.
